My Profile

SELF INTRODUCTION:

I am an academician with an experience of 27 years in the field of Education. I am the Dean of School of Science and Technology . I am also the Head of the Department and Assistant Professor in the Department of Food and Nutrition, School of Science and Technology, Vanita Vishram Women’s University, Surat. I have served as the Founder I/c Deputy Registrar and Dean of School of Vocational Studies in the University. I have served this institution for 15 years from 1995 to 2010 as a Lecturer in Sheth P.T. Mahila College of Arts and Home Science. The leadership strive in me gave me an opportunity to lead as the Principal of L.P. Savani School for 8 years. I was also the head of Countryside Institute of Training. I again joined Sheth P.T. Mahila College of Arts and Home Science as an Assistant Professor in 2019 and have served in the Coordination Committee of the University. I have a Doctorate in Applied Nutrition with Masters in Dietetics and Masters in Sports and Exercise Nutrition. I am UGC NET qualified. I have done various projects of Government and NGO’s in the field of Nutrition, Education and Women Empowerment. I extend my services to the community by being trustee and Office Bearer of many NGO’s
